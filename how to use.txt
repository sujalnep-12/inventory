First-Time Setup

Go to Categories
Add categories like:

Product: Laptop, Printer, Accessories, Parts
Service: Repair, Installation, Software, Maintenance
Expense: Rent, Salary, Transport, Internet, Purchase
Go to Products
Add every item you sell:

Product name
SKU if you have one
Cost price
Selling price
Current stock quantity
Low stock alert number
Go to Services
Add services you provide:

Service name
Standard price
Delivery cost, meaning your internal cost
Duration if useful
Daily Use

When you sell something, go to New Sale

Select customer name if needed
Add product or service line items
Quantity and price will calculate the total
If the customer paid fully, leave Amount paid blank
If partial payment, enter the paid amount
Product stock will reduce automatically after saving the sale.

When you spend money, go to Expenses
Add expenses like rent, tea, transport, internet, salary, product purchase, etc.

When you buy or correct stock, go to Stock
Use:

Restock when adding new stock
Remove stock for damaged/lost items
Set stock level when correcting count
Checking Business Records

Dashboard shows daily sales, expenses, profit, remaining balance, cash received, and low stock.
Reports lets you choose a date range.
Click Export XLSX to download your Excel report.
